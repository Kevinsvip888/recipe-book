# Guacamole recipe


## Ingredients

- 2 avocados
- 1 chili
- 1 lime
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 onion
- 1 Tbsp cilantro


## Instructions

- chop avocados
- chop onion
- chop chili
- squeeze lime
- add salt
- and mix well
- enjoy!
